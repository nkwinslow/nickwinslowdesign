I Love Product Design

I have been interested in technology my whole life. After graduating High School and serving a volunteer two year service mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints, I returned to start an interest in web development and design.

I have since attended school to learn how to code as well as design from DevMountain, a coding academy that specializes lin the accelerating learning of web technologies. Such technologies include HTML, Javascript, Angular, Node.JS, and more. Also while attending DevMountain, I was able to attend classes of User Experience and User Interface Design, learning the full design process from user interviews, sketching, low-fidelity wire-frames as well as high-fidelity mockups in Sketch. I love being able to think of an idea, design it, then take part in it's development.

In my free time I love to participate in the sport of Martial Arts Tricking. A sport consisting of various kicks, flips, and twists, Tricking has been a great way for me to de-stress from a productive days work. I have done work in Tricking for both Youtube and Photography.
